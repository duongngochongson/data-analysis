SQL eCommerce User Behavior Exploration

Analyzing user engagement and revenue trends on Google Analytics public dataset.

Approach

BigQuery public GA sample. Heavy use of UNNEST on nested hits & product, CTEs and window functions across 8 queries.

Key questions answered

  • Visits, pageviews, transactions for Jan–Mar 2017
  • Bounce rate per traffic source (Jul 2017)
  • Revenue by source — weekly & monthly (Jun 2017)
  • Avg pageviews: purchasers vs non-purchasers
  • Avg transactions / revenue per user
  • Cross-sell from "YouTube Men's Vintage Henley"
  • Cohort funnel: view → cart → purchase

Highlighted insight

March 2017 had the highest purchase rate (12.64%) and the most transactions; February peaked in add-to-cart engagement.

Retailer RFM Segmentation

Segmenting SuperStore customers by Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value to drive marketing campaigns.

Pipeline

  1. Pull transactions & segmentation lookup from Google Sheets
  2. Clean: drop duplicates, cancelled orders (InvoiceNo starts with C), negative qty/price
  3. Score R, F, M with pd.qcut quintiles → 3-digit RFM score
  4. Map to 7 named segments & aggregate
  5. Visualize with Seaborn & Squarify

Highlighted insight

Champions make up 19.2% of customers; Hibernating 15.9%. SuperStore should prioritize Recency & Frequency over Monetary to convert Potential Loyalists.
